Mission

The original web, as conceived by Tim Berners-Lee, is almost dead.

There’s just enough of it left so that we can fool ourselves into believing otherwise.

But maybe we shouldn’t mourn the dying web, maybe we should build something better instead.

What would that look like? It had to be a true internet of people. Something where everyone could say: we are the net.

How? That’s what we’re here to find out.
